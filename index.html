Rhizobial Symbiotic Gene Compendium

Comprehensive reference of symbiotic genes in Bradyrhizobium spp. and related rhizobia (Sinorhizobium, Rhizobium, Mesorhizobium, Azorhizobium and others), with verified literature citations and hyperlinks. Covers nodulation (nod/nol/noe), nitrogen fixation (nif/fix), hydrogen uptake (hup/hyp), type III secretion (T3SS/nop), and regulatory genes. Primary focus on Bradyrhizobium.

This resource is intended as a: Quick reference during genome annotation and comparative genomics Teaching resource for courses on biological nitrogen fixation and plant-microbe interactions Companion tool for the rhizobial symbiotic gene search and future phylogeny pipeline (in development) Please take note that this is a work in progress and the author does not take responsibility for any errors at the time of publication.

Conservation across diazotrophic Bradyrhizobium / rhizobia:  ● Universal present in all diazotrophic rhizobia  |  ● Common present in most; very few exceptions  |  ◐ Variable widespread but genus/clade-restricted  |  ○ Restricted strain- or species-specific
  • nod/nol/noe — Nod factor (LCO) biosynthesis, decoration, and export genes. nodABCDIJ are universal among nod-based nodulators; all others are accessory.
  • nif — Nitrogen fixation structural/biosynthetic genes. nifHDKENBASU are universally conserved across all diazotrophs.
  • fix — Rhizobia-specific symbiotic N₂ fixation genes. fixABCX and fixNOQPGHIS are universal in symbiotic rhizobia. etfSL are housekeeping ETF paralogs (Group I) distinct from the symbiotic FixA/FixB (Group II).
  • hup/hyp — Hydrogen uptake hydrogenase and maturation genes; variable — present in Hup⁺ strains only.
  • T3SS/nop — Type III secretion system and effectors; variable — widespread in Bradyrhizobium but not universal.
  • Regulatory/Other — σ⁵⁴ (rpoN) is universal; others are variable or Bradyrhizobium-specific.
